Remote Server Management

  • In the realm of IT infrastructure management, Linux servers hold a pivotal role in powering businesses with stability, security, and scalability. However, managing Linux servers effectively can be a daunting task, especially in environments where multiple servers are dispersed across various locations. This is where Linux Remote Server Management steps in as your indispensable ally.

Features of Remote Management :

  • Proactive Monitoring: - Our dedicated team of skilled engineers maintains continuous vigilance over connectivity and server services 24/7 from our central Network Operations Centre (NOC).
  • Maintenance and Auditing: Our responsibilities encompass monitoring event logs, scheduling maintenance tasks, managing patches, overseeing antivirus operations, monitoring backups, and more.
  • Comprehensive Safeguarding: As an integral aspect of our initial audit and proactive maintenance services, we offer server optimization and fortification, conduct thorough firewall audits, provide anti-virus and spam protection, and more to ensure complete protection.
  • Backup Solutions and Data Retrieval: We offer comprehensive technical support for devising backup and recovery strategies, along with configuration and continuous monitoring.
  • Certified Experts at Your Service: By partnering with us, your organization gains round-the-clock access to skilled and certified domain specialists who recognize the critical significance of uptime, security, and reliability.
  • Performance-Based SLAs: We guarantee a response time of 30 minutes and aim for issue resolution within 6 hours for all technical support incidents.
